STAFF POLICIES
Who they are, what they do, and how to deal with them.

Meet the Staff Team

Staff at Last Call RP are volunteers who keep the lights on and the peace between scenes. They're players too — they have characters, crews, and stories. The difference is they're also responsible for making sure everyone gets a fair shot at good RP. Treat them accordingly. Follow rule one, and do not reach out directly to staff with issues. Make use of the bug-reporting forum or open tickets.

1. Staff Roles

  • Director: Final say on all policy, direction, and appeals.
  • Lead: Oversees their departments.
  • Developer: They develop features and bug fix.
  • Staff: Handle a variety of things to keep things running smoothly.
  • Moderator: Are the first in line to look at tickets and elevate issues to where they need to be seen.

2. Tickets

  • If you have an issue, open a ticket, and a moderator will address it and determine where to escalate if needed.

3. Reporting a Player

  • Use the ticket system remember to include: your name, their name, what happened, when it happened, and evidence if available.
  • Don't report the same person multiple times for the same incident. One report, let staff handle it.
  • Don't file retaliatory reports (filing a report because someone reported you first)

4. Appealing Decisions

  • Bans, and warnings can all be appealed through the Discord ticket system.
  • Be specific and calm in your appeal. 'This is unfair' is not an argument. Explain what happened from your perspective.
  • A moderator will review the appeals, and will escalate to the required staff. Their decision is final.

5. Staff Conduct Standards

  • Staff are held to the same rules as everyone else — plus a bit more.
  • Staff cannot abuse their position for IC advantages (spawning items, teleporting unfairly, looking at player locations).
  • Staff are not immune from bans. Abuse of power is taken seriously.

6. Contacting Staff

  • Use the ticketing system.

7. Staff Availability

Staff are volunteers. Response times vary. Peak hours see faster responses — off-peak may take longer. If something is urgent and no staff are available, document it (screenshot/video) and please be patient.

Staff aren't here to police the fun — they're here to protect it. Work with them, not against them.
