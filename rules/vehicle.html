VEHICLE RULES
Drive like there's a cop watching. Because there probably is.

Why Vehicle Rules Matter

Cars are one of the most common sources of RP issues on any server.

1. Drive Realistically

  • Drive at speeds that make sense for the road and situation. Nobody in real life is doing 200mph through downtown. We have speed limits. Consult the penal code for specifics. Or ask a member of PD.
  • Obey traffic laws when there's no active RP reason not to. Red lights, stop signs, lane discipline, there are rules. PD will enforce them.

2. Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

  • Do NOT use your vehicle as a weapon.
  • Hit-and-runs on players require active, ongoing RP justification.
  • Ramming police vehicles during a chase must have a proportionate in-character reason.
  • Pinning someone on foot with a vehicle to rob/detain them is NOT allowed.

3. Vehicle Pursuits

  • Pursuit rules for LEOs are defined in the LEO SOP. Civilians: use good judgment and don't intentionally bait chases.
  • Helicopter griefing (hovering over a scene just to be annoying) is a violation.

4. Vehicle hiding in pursuit

  • If you escape the police, do not immediately park your vehicle in a garage. Give them a few minutes before parking it.

Your car is an extension of your character — treat it like one. A wheelman has a rep. Don't blow it on a random.
